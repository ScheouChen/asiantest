Fudan University, Shanghai, China

Scope

The Asian Workshop on First-Principles Electronic Structure Calculations is an international symposium, which started in 1998 in Tsukuba, and has since been held every year alternating in various locations in Asia. The Workshop focuses on the First-Principles Electronic Structure Calculations.

Covered Topics

  • Condensed matter physics
  • Materials science
