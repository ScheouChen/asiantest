Conference site

The Workshop will be held in Physics Hall, Jiangwan Campus, Fudan University (复旦大学江湾校区物理楼) .

Jiangwan Campus, Fudan University (复旦大学江湾校区)

Travel

Jiangwan campus is the campus where most of the science and engineering faculties of Fudan University are located. Jiangwan campus is about 5 kilometers away from the old campus (Handan campus) and Wujiaochang commercial district, about 45 kilometers away from Shanghai Pudong International Airport and about 36 kilometers away from Hongqiao International Airport. Guofan Road Station of Shanghai Metro Line 10 is right next to the entrance of Jiangwan Campus. The accommodation of all invited speakers will be provided at Shanghai Wujiaochang Boling hotel, which is about 1.7 kilometers away from the Physics Hall.
