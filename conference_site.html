Travel

Jiangwan campus is the campus where most of the science and engineering faculties of Fudan University are located. Jiangwan campus is about 5 kilometers away from the old campus (Handan campus) and Wujiaochang commercial district, about 45 kilometers away from Shanghai Pudong International Airport and about 36 kilometers away from Hongqiao International Airport. Guofan Road Station of Shanghai Metro Line 10 is right next to the entrance of Jiangwan Campus.

  • Jiangwan Campus of Fudan University is about an hour's drive from Shanghai Pudong International Airport (PVG) and Hongqiao International Airport.
  • Shanghai Wujiaochang Boling hotel is 1.7 kilometers away from the Physics Hall.
